This is what Abuja looks like

Not opinions. Verified reports from Nigerian newspapers, government records and residents — published in 2025 and 2026.

What residents told Daily Trust — January 2026

  • "Areas like Kubwa, Lugbe, Nyanya, Karu and Gwagwalada need intervention. The internal roads in these areas are in terrible condition." — FCT resident
  • Flash floods claimed 15 lives in Nyanya and Garki during the 2024 rainy season, submerging markets and bursting sewers
  • A single flood cleanup in Jabi cost ₦2.3 billion in 2024 — money that could have fixed preventive drainage
  • Kuje Area Council communities cut off from neighbouring towns every rainy season due to road collapse
  • Streetlights absent from Nyanya, Karu, Orozo and Karshi — residents appealed to the FCT Minister in December 2024
Sources: Daily Trust (Jan 2026) · Africa's a Country (Feb 2026) · PM News (Dec 2024)

How it works

Three steps. Maximum impact.
